Privacy Policy

About FlagAtlas

FlagAtlas (flagatlas.co.uk) is a free educational website providing interactive flag-based learning tools for children, teenagers, and families. It is operated as a personal project and provided free of charge with no advertising.

FlagAtlas does not collect, store, or share any personal information about its visitors. No accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking.

Data we collect

FlagAtlas does not collect personal data. We do not ask for your name, email address, age, location, or any other identifying information at any point.

We do not use analytics tools such as Google Analytics that track behaviour across the web. We do not serve advertisements. We do not sell or share any data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Local storage

Some FlagAtlas tools save progress and scores directly in your browser using localStorage — a standard browser feature that stores small amounts of data on your own device. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or any server.

The following data may be saved locally in your browser:

  • Your daily challenge guesses and streak count
  • Your speed quiz high score and games played
  • Your progress through fact card categories

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or local storage for flagatlas.co.uk.

Cookies

FlagAtlas does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Some cookies may be set by third-party services listed below (such as Google Fonts) as part of their normal operation, but these are limited to performance and delivery purposes and do not track you personally.

Third-party services

FlagAtlas uses a small number of third-party services to deliver the site:

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to load the typefaces used across the site. When your browser requests a font, your IP address may be visible to Google as part of that request. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

flagcdn.com

Flag images are loaded from flagcdn.com, a free public flag image service. Your IP address may be visible to this service when images are requested. We have no control over their data practices.

Netlify

FlagAtlas is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect standard server logs including IP addresses as part of hosting. Please refer to Netlify's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's privacy

FlagAtlas is designed to be safe for children. Because we do not collect any personal data and require no registration, the site is suitable for use by children of all ages without parental consent being required for data processing purposes.

Parents and guardians are welcome to contact us with any concerns about the site's content or data practices.
